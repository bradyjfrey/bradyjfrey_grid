A Common Green

Bently Reserve

Client Launch: August 2009
What I did
Art Direction
Lead Design
Logo/Branding Design

Designed as a marketing focus for an up-and-coming not-for-profit, A Common Green is a website driven heavily on social media. As a whole, the Bently Reserve allowed free-reign on all areas of marketing, and gave me the ability to streamline the website with a real time Google Ads, Twitter, and Facebook advertising blitz.

Branding, Identity, Web Design, Print Design, iPhone optimization and all visual graphics were designed and approved by me, with demographic input and tradtional marketing support from Event & Sales Manager Vivian Perez of the Bently Reserve, Content Editorial and Copy Writing by Doug Hahn, PR Management from Heidi Pettit & Deirdre Holbrook of Vie PR and the exceptional management team of The Orchard Hotels in San Francisco. The overwhelming turnout has all but guaranteed an annual event!

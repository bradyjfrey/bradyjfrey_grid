Captain Bently

Chris Bently

Client Launch: September 2008
What I did
Art Direction
Lead Design

The custom design for Chris Bently’s Private Birthday Party at Forbes Island. Design includes website, print invitation, email invitation custom food and drink menus, flag, and map for buried “treasures”. The original intention was a simple invitation, but the idea came up to make it look as if the restaurant/island was hijacked by our pirate crew (costumes required), and I stepped up the design to market a feel of a professional, authentic, event. All Illustrator, InDesign, and photorealistic manipulation ala Photoshop.

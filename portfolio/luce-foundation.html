Luce Foundation

Academy Studios

Client Launch: February 2006
What I did
Logo/Branding
Lead Design
Front-End Development
Interaction Design

The Branding, Kiosk, & Website Design for the grand opening of the Luce Foundation. Academy Studios managed and led exhibit design for this opening, handing off technical design requests to me. The original specifications I built were a tableless, semantic, handicap accessible design that utilized XML data so it could be shared across various devices in real time throughout the Smithsonian. Eventually project scope changed, and much to my dismay, the final design was outsourced to Mediatrope for a subtle design built with Coldfusion, and using older code.

Although my design never reached final production, it was featured in Logo Lounge 3 as a premier Identity design.
